OLUWATOBI SOLOMON OYEYEMI

Oyeyemi Solomon Oluwatobi simply known by his stage name Tobi, from the City of Osogbo, Osun State was born and brought up in Yola Adamawa State, North Eastern Nigeria. Tobi is a Christian, Gospel artist and a graduate of Geology from Salem University, Lokoja.

He discovered his passion for music and started singing in his Local Children Church Choir at the age of 9 years and have been progressing from there on. Tobi is a Nigerian Afro gospel, Afro beat, and Hip-Hop, gospel rapper and singer, songwriter, recording and performing artist. Ever since in his musical career, he started professionally with the release of his first Collaboration True Story ft Joerex in the year 2015 a track which won the Exousia Gospel Music Award 2017/2018 as (Hip Hop and Rap Song) of the year. He also dropped his first official single FOR ME, produced by Selah Studio, describing how great our God is and his unconditional love.

One of his major achievement is “Tobi's room”. Tobi’s Room is a platform which was initiated by Tobi in 2019 to push his music and also foster the growth of Gospel Music around the Northern part of Nigeria. Together with his team were able to organize a live recording session in December 2019, and Tobi’s Room Acoustic Live Session 2020 were he featured artist like Courage Ayim, Joerex, Mori, Olaitan Tobi, Vincent Kieran, Ndatam Cyril, Mo-Man Eremo and Prince Mathias. He is currently working on his EP Album which schedule to drop before the year runs out.
Other than his music career, he is a practicing Geologist. Hobbies outside music are: playing and watching football, playing video games, traveling and meeting new people.
